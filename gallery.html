A look at work from the factotum world — raw clay, dried flowers, ink and watercolour. A little of what's possible when you give yourself room to explore.

materia botanica · 2024

clay & dried flowers

This project blends raw clay and dried flowers, exploring fragility, preservation and the transformation of nature. The contrast between durable clay and delicate blooms reflects nature's balance between resilience and impermanence.

faces that hold the seasons

Each vessel is hand-shaped from raw clay, its quiet expression left deliberately imperfect. Dried botanicals turn every piece into a small, slow still-life.

resilience & impermanence

The durability of fired clay set against the fragility of dried flowers — a study in how things last, and how they don't.
