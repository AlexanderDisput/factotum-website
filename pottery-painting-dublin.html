pottery painting in dublin

Spend a calm couple of hours painting your own pottery in the heart of Dublin's Liberties. No experience needed — just pick a piece, pick your colours, and make something that's yours.

what to expect

Choose a ready-made bisque piece — a mug, bowl, vase or little dish — and bring it to life with colour, pattern and glaze. We fire everything for you and let you know when it's ready to collect.

  • All paints, glazes & tools provided
  • Friendly guidance throughout
  • Firing & collection included
