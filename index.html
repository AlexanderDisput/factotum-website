what you can make

three ways to get creative

Each session is relaxed and beginner-friendly. Come solo, bring a friend, or book the space for a group.

pottery painting

Choose a bisque piece and bring it to life with colour and pattern. We glaze and fire it for you to collect later.

≈ 2 hrs · all levels

sewing

Learn the machine from scratch and stitch a project you'll actually use — a tote, a cushion, a little something of your own.

≈ 2.5 hrs · beginner-friendly

textile crafts

Experiment with natural dyeing, weaving and mixed-media textile play, guided by the seasons and what's at hand.

≈ 2 hrs · all levels
