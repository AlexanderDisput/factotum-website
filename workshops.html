workshops

sessions for curious hands.

Every workshop is relaxed, guided and beginner-friendly. No experience needed — just curiosity. Sessions run for individuals, pairs and small groups, right through to corporate team-building events — in the studio, or we come to you.

pottery painting

Pick a ready-made bisque piece — a mug, bowl, vase or little dish — and make it yours with colour, pattern and glaze. We handle the firing and let you know when it's ready to collect.

  • Choose from a range of bisqueware
  • All paints, glazes & tools provided
  • Firing & collection included
≈ 2 hours all levels from €TBC

sewing

Get comfortable at the machine and leave with something you made yourself. We start with the basics and build up to a finished project — a tote bag, cushion cover or simple garment.

  • Machines, fabric & threads provided
  • Learn threading, seams & finishing
  • Take home a completed project
≈ 2.5 hours beginner-friendly from €TBC

textile crafts

A playful, exploratory session in the spirit of materia botanica — natural dyeing, weaving and mixed-media textile work, led by the seasons and the materials at hand.

  • Seasonal natural materials
  • Hands-on dyeing & weaving techniques
  • A keepsake to take home
≈ 2 hours all levels from €TBC
